Lena, a thirty-year-old woman with an intellectual disability, begins working at a Goodwill. There, she meets Bob, a forty-something-year-old who also has an intellectual disability. They begin dating and quickly fall in love. A few months pass and Bob asks Lena to marry him.
Lena's mother, Ella, is terrified of her daughter's newfound independence. Lena's father, Lou, however, is glad that Lena has found love.
